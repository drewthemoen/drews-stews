    Stew Analysis



    Curry is a term for a very broad category of dishes that come from South Asia, and has since gained popularity around the world. There's anything from Indian curries to Thai curries, and anything else. Almost all curries are going to have some type of sauce, and have their own unique blend of spices. This might be turmeric, cumin, coriander, etc...


    The wikipedia page mentions proto-curries, which may not have incorporated some spices like chili pepper. These were brought by the Portuguese during the 16th century, so they wouldn't have been part of the blend yet.


    Curry is another category of stew that is easy to find enjoyable verions of by virtue of the fact that there are so many variations out there. You can have spicy or mild, meat-based or vegetarian, brothier or thicker, etc. It gives more margin for customization than most.
