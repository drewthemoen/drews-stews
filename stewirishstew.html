    Stew Analysis



    When I think of the word stew, this particular stew may be the first thing that pops up in my mind. As far as stews go, Irish stew stands out as a very comforting stew. You've got all of the classic stew ingredients you'd think of: meats, potatoes, carrots, and a broth of choice. If I were to come down with an illness, this would be one of the go-tos.



    The potatoes that go into this stew remain a staple of Ireland, and as you can probably tell by how present that ingredient is in several other stews on this site, they are incredibly versatile. As Samwise Gamgee once said, "Potatoes! Boil 'em, mash 'em, stick 'em in a stew!"

    If chili didn't exist, this would probably be my favorite stew, too. It's savory, it's awesome, it's still got a good amount of broth, and there's a ton of regional varieties. It's just everything a stew should be.
